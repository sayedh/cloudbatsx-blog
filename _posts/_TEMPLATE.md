Open with the human problem: who had it and why it mattered. Then what you built or did, then
what it let them do. Keep the stack as evidence in service of the outcome — never the headline.

## A section heading

Write the body in Markdown. Inline `code` and fenced code blocks render in JetBrains Mono:

```bash
aws s3 ls
```
